My Journey

In August 2018, I left my home just north of Columbus and started my CS degree at UC. A year later, I started my first Co-Op at Siemens PLM Software, a half-hour drive away from campus. I worked on an ML Research Team. After another semester of school in the Spring of 2020, I did another Co-Op at Siemens in Summer 2020, where I worked on a Full-Stack web team.

Co-Op Program Participation

Through the Co-Op program, I've been able to explore my interests in a porfessional environment to get a better idea of what I want to do when I leave school.

Learning and Growth through Co-Op

When I started Co-Op, I thought I already knew everything and a job would be easy. To my surprise, what I knew was only the beginning of what I needed to know. I learned a lot on the job through mentors and making mistakes and gaining experience.

If I could start all over again

I'd start doing LeetCode earlier, and make more interesting and fun personal projects. As well, I would have accepted a different offer that I had at the time rather than work at Siemens.

Goals for the future

With the experience and knowledge that I now have, I want to gain more experience at a large tech company in a tech hub in the future in my following Co-Ops.
